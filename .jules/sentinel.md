## 2024-04-13 - Missing HTML Sanitization in Email Templates
**Vulnerability:** The application was vulnerable to HTML Injection and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) within the email templates, specifically in the Contact Form submission route (`src/app/api/contact/route.ts`). User input was directly interpolated into HTML strings without sanitization. Additionally, email headers (`subject`, `replyTo`) lacked sanitization against CRLF injection in the `sendEmail` utility.
**Learning:** Even though the email client might have some XSS protection, rendering unsanitized user input in HTML emails can lead to malicious payloads being executed or formatting being broken. Furthermore, failing to strip newlines in email headers can allow attackers to inject arbitrary email headers.
**Prevention:** Implement a central sanitization utility (`src/lib/html-sanitizer.ts`) with `escapeHtml` for HTML body content and `stripNewlines` for email headers. Always sanitize user inputs before placing them into HTML templates, and always strip newlines from user-provided email header fields.
