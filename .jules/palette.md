## 2024-04-01 - Descriptive ARIA labels for Icon/Action Buttons
**Learning:** Action buttons that mix icons and very short text (e.g. `<Heart /> Save`, `<Share2 /> Share`) are technically not "icon-only" but can still be highly ambiguous for screen reader users. The visible text alone ("Save") lacks the context of "Save business", while the presence of the decorative lucide-react SVGs can lead to redundant or confusing readouts if not hidden.
**Action:** When working on action bars or icon+text buttons, always explicitly hide decorative icons with `aria-hidden="true"` and override the short visible text with a full-sentence descriptive `aria-label` (e.g., `aria-label="Save business"`) to provide complete semantic context for assistive technologies. Add `aria-pressed` for toggle states.
